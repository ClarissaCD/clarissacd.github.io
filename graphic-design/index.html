Crazy, Stupid, Twenties

Cover art designed for "Crazy Stupid Twenties", a podcast hosted by Alice Nguyen. Nguyen's podcast entails the trials and tribulations of being in your twenties. I was tasked to create a simple cover for her podcast that was feminine and fun. Her podcast can be found on Spotify.

La Vanguardia

Logo created for La Vanguardia, a latinx organization at Hamilton College. La Vanguardia pushes for support of Latinx students on campus while also establishing various cultural traditions that celebrate their members' cultural backgrounds. I was tasked to create a lively logo that represented their organization.

LULAC

I created a design for Southern Methodist University's Multicultural Greek Council to use for embroideries and letterheads. SMU changed their university logo in 2021 and the MGC wanted to update their logo to include SMU's. I was tasked to create a simple design that could be easily scaled and followed the brand guidelines of the university.
