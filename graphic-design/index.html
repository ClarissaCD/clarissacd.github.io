Crazy, Stupid, Twenties

I designed the cover art for Crazy Stupid Twenties, a podcast hosted by Alice Nguyen, which explores the trials and tribulations of navigating life in your twenties. Using Procreate, I crafted a design that reflects the podcast’s feminine and fun tone. The cover is featured on platforms like Spotify.

La Vanguardia

I designed a vibrant logo for La Vanguardia, a Latinx organization at Hamilton College dedicated to supporting Latinx students and celebrating cultural traditions. Using Procreate, I created a lively visual identity that reflects the organization’s mission and spirit. This design was later used for stickers and flyers to promote their initiatives on campus.

LULAC

I designed a logo for Southern Methodist University’s Multicultural Greek Council to be used for embroideries and letterheads. Following SMU’s rebranding in 2021, the MGC sought a refreshed logo that incorporated the new university logo while adhering to SMU’s brand guidelines. I was tasked with creating a simple, scalable design suitable for various uses, from printed materials to embroidered apparel.
