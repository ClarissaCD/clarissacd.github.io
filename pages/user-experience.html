Crazy, Stupid, Twenties

Alice is a close friend, "Crazy, Stupid, Twenties" was a podcast and personal project she started in college. On the podcast she discussed topics such as mental health, well-being and her experience in college. I created the cover for her show using ProCreate.

La Vanguardia

La Vanguardia is a collegiate organization for Latin American students at Hamilton College in New York. I created this logo for them so that they could create stickers and other promotional material. The logo was created using ProCreate.

LULAC

The LULAC (League of United Latin American Citizens) chapter at Southern Methodist University wanted an emblem for their council. This emblem was created using Adobe Illustrator.
